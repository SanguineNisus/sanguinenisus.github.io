Removing "Busyness" from Your Business, Deliver Outstanding Patient Care!

SaaS Product Improvement | Timeline: 4 Months | My roles: Product Design, Prototyping/Wireframing, UX Design, UX Research

A software platform designed to streamline and enhance the operations of dental practices by providing tools for appointment scheduling, billing, data analytics, and patient communication.

Single practices often have difficulty managing efficient time for patients and their unique procedures while determining effective ways to have a consistent, streamlined approach for earning revenue. That's where SikkaPrime™ provides an intuitive and user-friendly interface that simplifies complex dental practice management tasks, ensuring efficient workflow for both dental professionals and staff while also delivering a visually appealing and cohesive design that aligns with the healthcare industry's standards and fosters strong B2B trust.


Design Overview:

SikkaPrime™ empowers dental professionals and aims to prioritize user needs by creating an intuitive and visually appealing interface that streamlines dental practice management tasks while adhering to industry standards for professionalism and accessibility. Through user-centered design, efficient navigation, and clear data visualization, the platform seeks to enhance usability and scalability for dental professionals.

The Client

Solo dental practices and dental service organizations

The Challenge

Designing a SaaS platform for solo dental practices and select dental service organizations needs to factor dental professionals and the limited time they have outside of doing dental procedures. To determine how this core challenge is addressed, we need to factor some pivotal questions:

 • How do we balance robust features without overwhelming users?
 • How do we allow dental practices to provide options for patients?
 • How can we balance aesthetics with accessibility in design?
 • How do we present data both attractively and understandably?
 • How can we ensure the design remains scalable?

Building The Foundation For A Sophisticated SaaS Experience for Solo Dental Practices and Select Dental Service Organizations

The overhaul update for Optimizer™ Enterprise rolled out in July 2022. This handy SaaS contains and organizes patient data with consideration of the dental practices utlizing the program.

What I've Learned & Personal Thoughts

Analyzing the user feedback for SikkaPrime, I've learned that while the product has made significant strides in improving productivity and customer support, there's room for enhancement in user onboarding and interface design. Balancing advanced features with simplicity will be key to ensuring both beginners and experienced users can fully benefit from the platform's capabilities. Additionally, prioritizing ongoing UI/UX improvements based on user feedback can help SikkaPrime™ continue to evolve and provide a more seamless experience.
